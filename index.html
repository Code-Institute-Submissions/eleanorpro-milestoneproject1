Welcome

The Dice People

Whitley Bay's first board game cafe

NOW OPEN

The Dice People is the first board game cafe to open in Whitley Bay. We provide a space for gamers to eat, drink and play in a friendly environment.

We have a collection of over 100 board games, from the classics like Monopoly and Scrabble, to the modern such as Azul and Wingspan.

As well as a varied and inclusive menu and a fully licensed bar, we also host a range of fun and exciting events. Check out our events timetable below or contact us using the link above to see whats happening in the future.

Our staff are all keen board gamers and would be happy to help explain rules and make recommendations to new and experienced board gamers.
