Prices

Booking a table will cost £3 per person for up to three hours of use. This includes use of any of our board games.

For longer board games, or to book a table for a full day, there will be a total charge of £25 for groups of up to 10 players. For bigger groups, please contact us to discuss booking.

Food and drinks prices can be viewed on our menu.

Attending one of our events costs £4, which includes a free drink.
